Humanoid Onion Companion

This recipe will save you a lot of time and frustration

You should make this recipe if you want a kitchen helper who can help you with instructions

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Love
  • Spells & Magic
  • Pot

Steps

  1. locate a suitable onion
  2. Get a spellbook from your local wizardry supply store
  3. Follow the instructions in the book
  4. Become friends with the humanoid onion
  5. Lastly... Enjoy never having to read cooking instructions again
