Turtle Soup

This meaty soup recipe will save you money and fill your tummy!

Make this delicious recipe when you are hungry and want to get rid of your pet turtle

Ingredients

  • Fresh Turtle
  • Soup broth
  • Vegetables
  • Bacon Strips

Steps to follow:

  1. First of all, Boil the damn turtle
  2. Then Boil your Vegetables with your turtle inside of the soup broth
  3. After that fry some bacon
  4. Plate your meal by placing your turtle on a plate, then add some soup broth and vegetables along with the bacon strips on top
  5. Lastly... Enjoy your flavorful meal!
