M n M Pizza Recipe

This recipe is a family treaure! make it whenever you want something to spice up your life

I made this recipe for my family on a cold December night a few years ago, I loved it, they hated it.

Ingredients

  • MnM candy
  • Cold Pizza
  • Empty fridge

Steps

  1. Find a reason to make this pizza
  2. Prepare your cold pizza by hitting it with a stick to get it soft
  3. Shove in your MnM candy onto the pizza with a lot of force
  4. Don't enjoy your sad pizza
