Spaghetti Jello with hotdogs and olives

I first tried making this recipe from a recommendation my mother gave me.

If the only ingredients you have in your fridge are gelatin, spaghetti O's, Olives, and hotdogs. Then make this IMMEDIATELY, it will satisfy you.

Ingredients

  • Hotdogs
  • Gelatin powder
  • Olives
  • Spaghetti O's

Steps

  1. Firstly, get a weird shaped bowl to prepare this nasty dish
  2. Then, cook your Spaghetti O's
  3. After that, let your Spaghetti O's become cold. Then put them in the Gelatin mixture
  4. Cut up your Hotdogs
  5. Place the cut up Hotdogs and the Olives on top of your jelly spaghetti
  6. Enjoy!
